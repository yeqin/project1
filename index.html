Qin Studio



The space offers digital fabrication and traditional craft opportunities for members, staffs, and faculty. Through coursework, workshops, and studio hours, the lab serves as a place of experimentation and collaboration with an emphasis in learning new technologies to empower participants to design, make, prototype, and build across the disciplines. Become a member or purchase class packs and have access to a wide variety of equipment, tools, and open space to work on your project.



What we have

Equipment
3D Printers, 3D Scanner, Vinyl Cutter, Soldering Irons, Laser Cutter, Hand Tools
Software
Fusion 360, z Brush, Mesh Mixer, Sculptris, Maya, Cinema 4D, AutoCAD, Sketch Up, Arduino, Processing, Python, Adobe Creative Suite, Cura, Slicer, and more.
